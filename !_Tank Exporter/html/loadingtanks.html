Loading a tank.

This is done by right clicking on a tank in the tier list to the right of the render window.
A small context window will open with 2 options.. Load This.. and Reload Textures.
NOTE: If Tank Exporter finds a tank in res_mods, it will load the tank from that location and NOT the PKG files.
This includes any textures, visual and primitive files. TE does this to preview any geometry or texture changes.
Never export FBX from a tank loaded from the res_mods unless you have NEVER changed the chassis!
Because of the way Tank Exporter modifies the Chassis.visual_processed file, it loses the changes to the Marker locations.
Re-exporting a previously modified chassis primitive will break the FBX.


Click Load This.. to load a tank in to Tank Exporter.
You can also load the crashed models if you wish.
Tank Exporter will work exactly the same when exporting and importing FBX files.

Tank exporter will find and loaded every thing it can find that belongs to this tank.
After this search, a panel will open with a list of items it found for the tank.

You can also Reload Textures.
This is handy if you are painting the DDS files and want to preview them in TE

You can also load the tanks description from the WoT API.
A window will open showing the info for that tank.

 


There may or may not be multiple items for the Turret and Gun depending on what tank it is.

Select the items you wish to load and Click Continue.
If the Tank has a skin version, you will have the option to select those components.

Tank Exporter will load the geometry and textures for the different components of the tank.

The labeling of components does not mean they are the top level ones in a research tree.
Gun_01 is not necessarily better than Gun_02.
