Moving around in Tank Exporter:

Press the left mouse button to orbit the look at point.

To zoom in and out, press the right mouse button.

To move the look at point in X Z, press the middle mouse button.
Also you can move the look at point by pressing SHIFT and left mouse buttons at the same time.

To move the look at point Up and Down, press CTRL and the left mouse buttons at the same time.

Pressing SHIFT or CTRL always shows the center cross hair. This is the point you are looking at.
By looking at the lines of the cross hair, its easy to place where you want to look anywhere in the render window.
Loading a tank resets the view to a comfortable range.

Pressing N will show the surface normals.. There are 3 view modes.. Off / By Face / By Vertex.
By Face averages the 3 normals for that polygon face.

Pressing L will show or hide the 3 light positions in the scene. The RED colored ball is the light that orbits when you press F3.

If you turn on shadows under File/Settings, there is also a setting for which light will be used to calculate the shadows.
